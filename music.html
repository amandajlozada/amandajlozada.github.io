Lonesome Joan

Following in the footsteps of songwriters like Patti Smith, Phil Elverum, and Joni Mitchell, Lonesome Joan’s songwriting straddles a thin line between reality and myth.

These are deeply felt songs of dream and grief, sung in an oaky contralto.

Marked by emotionally affecting lyricism, lush arrangements, and delicate fingerpicking, this is music with feelings way bigger than your stomach.

Cats On Film

Boston-based duo Cats On Film started playing together in 2019, after spending their first meeting awestruck at the guitar solo in the middle of "Witchita Lineman".

A self-described "sad-songwriter supergroup", their songs eloquently articulate the trivial victories and raw defeats of daily life.

This is a project founded on 50/50 collaboration, both performers arranging, producing, singing, drumming, or hopping on guitar, keys and bass whenever it's needed.
