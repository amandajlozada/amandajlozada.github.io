Pricing Philosophy

I will always be more interested in building a healthy, sustainable working relationship than almost any other aspect of a job.

Interested in my work, or have a project you're eyeing me for, but uncertain about affordability?

Reach out, and we can figure out a fair compensatory structure together.

Sound Work

I’ve built up a significant skillset in audio production (recording, mixing, and rough mastering) over the last decade.

I relish a challenge, and a certain amount of dirty work - from cleaning up noisy audio, to recording in acoustically challenging spaces, to working with atypical equipment and atypical instruments, I love making weird things work.

I'm also delighted to work with performers as a recording or mixing engineer - it gives me an enormous amount of pleasure to aid people in the precise pursuit of their creative vision, and I am a patient, passionate, and deliberate collaborator.

Performance

Check out my music page, and see if you'd be interested in booking any of my bands, or are interested in working with me on performing something else entirely.

While I mostly perform live as a musician, I'm adaptive and excited to do other kinds of live perfomance, especially ones which are narrative or poetic in nature.

I'd also be thrilled to work with you as a vocal performer for your audio drama, film voiceover, or sound project.

Music Commissions

Need a musical collaborator/sound designer for your podcast, short film, poetry reading, livestream, or audio birthday card? I'd love to help!

I'm a longtime musical improvisor and would love the opportunity to use my instruments to build sound worlds for your creative work to live in.

I'm particularly intersted in supporting other artists in making their work come alive - and if you like my work, but don't yet have a project to hang it on, let's talk about it and see if we can make something together.

Creative Workshops/Consulting

Having trouble getting started on your own creative projects? Want to host a small workshop for your friends, event space, or job?

I love getting the opportunity to work with people of all ages on breaking through their creative blocks, whether they're established artists, or just starting out.

Editing

Need a second set of eyes on your short story, essay, poem, website, or song?

It's a pleasure for me to help you get to the heart of what you want to say. If you're not certain about sending your work to me, drop me a line, and let's talk a little about what you're looking for.
